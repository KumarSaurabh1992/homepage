About Me

Hello, I am a Member Technical Staff at SankhyaSutra Labs Pvt. Ltd. My current work involves building of particle codes to be coupled with the in-house Lattice Boltzmann codes for simulation of dilute system flows and efficient DSMC codes. To parallelize these codes, I use MPI and openMP library.

I completed my Bachelors from IIT (BHU), Varanasi, India in Chemical Enginnering. Having been fascinated by the simulations in the field of Computational Fluid Dynamics and Scientific Computing, I pursued my Masters in Industrial Mathematics and Scientific Computing from IIT Madras, wherein I got opportunity to study and complete my Master Thesis at RWTH Aachen, Germany through prestigious DAAD scholarship.

I am looking forward for an opportunity to delve deep in the field of Computational Fluid Dynamics.
